How to use the Payment Button

The payment button is just a suggestion. The important thing is the URL we are providing you. This URL contains your public token and a special parameter that you can define by your own. This special parameter will be returned to the Redirect URL after a successful subscription, and will also be available for you in every POST message that PayParrot notify to your server.

The last parameter that goes in the URL is external_id, wich you can fill with your own user's id or and internal subscription id.
